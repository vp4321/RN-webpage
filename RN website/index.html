ABOUT US

We, the People of RADIO NITroz of NIT Durgapur, strive to make a difference in the hectic life of college by bringing fun events and help people learn better with a calm mind because “Life is to live”.RADIO NITroz was formed in the year 2006 by our brilliant seniors. RADIO NITroz was the 3rd successful LAN/Internet based radio among all IITs and NITs. We carry out different events throughout the year, enthralling people with Joie de vivre. Our Jukebox event arena during AAROHAN (Techno - Management Fest of NIT Durgapur) is a much sought attraction of the fest . We also have a bunch of events during RECSTACY (Now NITYAMINI) which is the Cultural Fest of NIT Durgapur. We believe to start small but to work as a team to make sure that every event is a milestone and we leave no stone unturned in the process.
